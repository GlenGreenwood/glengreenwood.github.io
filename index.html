Photosynthesis:

 Overall equation: 6 CO₂ + 6 H₂O → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6 O₂
  1. Materials are absorbed, etc.
  2. Light Reaction
  3. Calvin Cycle
  4. Products are released or moved for more processing

Respiration:

 Overall equation: C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6 O₂ → 6 CO₂ + 6 H₂O Which powers ATP Production
  1. Glycolysis
  2. Substrate Level Phosphorilation
  3. TCA Cycle (Krebs Cycle)
  4. Oxidative Phosphorilation
